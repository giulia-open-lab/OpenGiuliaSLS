Developing your own simulator
=============================

If you want to develop your own simulator, and there's no intention to merge it into the main repository, it's not that
important that all code-style guidelines are followed.

Even though we highly recommend that you follow them, we understand that sometimes it's not possible, so feel free to
develop your simulator as you wish.

An important thing to take into account, is that new features or code changes may be merged into the ``main`` branch.
Since your branch diverges from this one, it's important that you keep an eye on those changes, and merge them into
your branch as soon as possible, and solving any conflicts that may arise.
